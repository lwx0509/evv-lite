1. Introduction

This guide is for caregivers who use the Visiting Systems app to check in and out of client visits, record notes, and track their schedule.

Visiting Systems is a web app — there is nothing to download. You access it through your phone's browser at visitingsystems.com. Your agency admin will give you your login credentials.

Tip: Add visitingsystems.com to your phone's home screen for quick access. In your browser, tap Share → Add to Home Screen (iPhone) or the menu → Add to Home Screen (Android).

2. Logging In

1
Open your browser and go to visitingsystems.com
2
Enter your email address and password
These were provided by your agency administrator.
3
Tap Log In
You will be taken directly to your visit schedule.
Password issues? Contact your agency office. They can reset your password. Do not share your login with anyone else.

3. The Home Screen

After logging in you will see your upcoming and recent visits grouped by date. Each visit card shows:

  • Client name
  • Client address
  • Scheduled start and end time
  • Visit status
StatusWhat it means
UpcomingScheduled but not yet started
In ProgressYou have checked in — visit is active
CompletedVisit is fully checked out
DeclinedYou declined this shift

4. Checking In

Check in when you arrive at the client's location, before you begin providing care.

1
Find the visit on your home screen
2
Tap the visit to expand it
3
Tap Check In
The app will record your GPS location and the exact time.
4
The visit status changes to In Progress
Your check-in time is now saved.
Important: You must be at the client's address when you check in. The system records your GPS location as part of Electronic Visit Verification (EVV). Checking in from a different location will create an exception flag.
No GPS? Make sure your browser has permission to access your location. If you see a location prompt, tap Allow.

5. Checking Out

Check out immediately when you finish your shift and are about to leave the client's home.

1
Find the In Progress visit on your home screen
2
Tap Check Out
3
Add any visit notes if needed (see Section 8)
4
Provide your signature (see Section 6)
5
Tap Confirm Check-Out
The visit will move to Completed.
Important: Always check out before leaving. Visits that are not checked out appear as overdue in your agency's system and may affect your payroll.

6. Signature

Some visits require a client or caregiver signature at check-out to confirm services were delivered. If your agency requires this:

  1. A signature pad will appear during check-out.
  2. Hand your phone to the client (or sign yourself, depending on your agency's policy).
  3. Use a finger to draw the signature on the pad.
  4. Tap Accept to confirm.
Tip: If the signature is incorrect, tap Clear and try again before tapping Accept.

7. Declining a Shift

If you cannot make a scheduled visit, you should decline it as early as possible so your agency can arrange coverage.

1
Find the upcoming visit on your home screen
2
Tap Decline Shift
3
Enter a reason
This is required so your agency understands why coverage is needed.
4
Tap Confirm
The visit will be flagged for your coordinator to reassign.
Note: Declining a visit does not remove it from the schedule — it flags it for your coordinator to follow up. Always also call your office to inform them directly.

8. Adding Visit Notes

You can add notes to a visit during check-out or after a visit is completed. Notes are visible to your agency coordinators.

Use notes to document:

  • Changes in the client's condition
  • Tasks completed or not completed
  • Client requests or concerns
  • Any incidents or unusual events
Tip: Good visit notes protect both you and the client. When in doubt, document it.

9. Viewing Visit History

Scroll down on your home screen to see completed visits from the past 7 days. Each completed visit shows your actual check-in and check-out times.

For older visit history, contact your agency office.

10. Troubleshooting

ProblemWhat to do
Can't log inDouble-check your email spelling and password. Contact your office if you're still locked out.
Check In button not appearingRefresh the page. Make sure you're logged in and the visit is showing as Upcoming.
Location permission errorGo to your phone's browser settings and allow location access for visitingsystems.com.
Visit not showing on my screenPull to refresh or reload the page. If still missing, contact your office — the visit may not have been scheduled yet.
Page won't loadCheck your internet connection. Try switching between Wi-Fi and mobile data.
Accidentally checked in at the wrong locationContact your office immediately so they can note the exception.

11. Getting Help

For app-related questions, contact your agency office first — they can resolve most issues.

For technical problems that your office cannot resolve, email support@visitingsystems.com and include:

  • Your name and the name of your agency
  • A description of the problem
  • A screenshot if possible
