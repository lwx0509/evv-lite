1. Introduction

Welcome to Visiting Systems, an Electronic Visit Verification (EVV) and home care management platform designed for home health agencies. This guide is for Subscriber Admins — agency owners, office managers, or coordinators who manage day-to-day operations from the admin dashboard.

As a Subscriber Admin you can create and manage clients and caregivers, schedule visits, monitor real-time compliance, export payroll, generate invoices, and configure your agency settings.

Tip: Caregivers access a separate mobile-friendly interface at the same URL. You do not need to send them to a different site.

2. Logging In

Navigate to visitingsystems.com and enter your email and password. The system will automatically direct you to the admin dashboard.

Forgotten password

Contact your system administrator to have your password reset. Password resets are handled from the System Admin panel.

Note: Visiting Systems uses secure session tokens that expire after inactivity. If you are redirected to the login page, simply log in again — your data is never lost.

4. Managing Clients

Go to Clients in the sidebar. Each client record holds contact information, address, payer type, and geographic coordinates used for EVV location verification.

Adding a client

  1. Click Add Client.
  2. Enter the client's name, address, and payer type (Medicaid, Private Pay, etc.).
  3. Click Save.

Editing a client

Click the client's name in the list to open their profile. Update any fields and click Save.

Tip: The client's address is used to verify caregiver check-in location via GPS. Make sure the address is accurate.

5. Managing Caregivers

Go to Caregivers in the sidebar. Caregiver records include contact information, employee ID, timezone, and login credentials.

Adding a caregiver

  1. Click Add Caregiver.
  2. Enter name, email address, employee ID (optional), and timezone.
  3. Set a temporary password — share it with the caregiver and ask them to keep it safe.
  4. Click Save.

Caregiver access

Once added, caregivers can log in at visitingsystems.com with their email and password. On mobile devices they are directed to the caregiver mobile interface; on desktop they see a simplified caregiver dashboard.

6. Scheduling Visits

Visits can be created from New Visit in the sidebar or directly from the Schedule tab.

Creating a visit

  1. Select the client.
  2. Select the caregiver.
  3. Set the scheduled start and end date/time.
  4. Click Create Visit.

The visit will appear immediately in the schedule and in the caregiver's mobile app.

Editing or cancelling a visit

Open the visit from the Schedule or Week View and click Edit or Cancel Visit. Cancelled visits are removed from the caregiver's queue.

Note: Editing the scheduled time of an in-progress visit (one where the caregiver has already checked in) will not affect the actual check-in time recorded for EVV purposes.

7. Visit Monitoring & Exceptions

The Alerts tab surfaces visits that need attention in real time:

  • Missed check-in — caregiver has not checked in and the scheduled start time has passed.
  • Overdue check-out — visit is in progress but the scheduled end time has passed.

The Exceptions tab lists all flagged visits for formal review. Flags include location mismatches, late arrivals, and declined shifts. Each exception can be reviewed and resolved with a note.

8. Payroll Export

Go to Payroll in the sidebar. Select a date range and click Export CSV. The file includes:

  • Caregiver name and employee ID
  • Client name
  • Actual check-in and check-out times
  • Total hours worked

Import the CSV into your payroll software (QuickBooks, ADP, Paychex, etc.).

Tip: Only visits with a completed check-out appear in the payroll export. Remind caregivers to check out at the end of every visit.

9. Invoicing & Billing

Go to Invoices to generate invoices for payers. Select a client, date range, and payer — the system will calculate billable hours from completed visits. Click Generate Invoice to create a record you can export or share.

The Billing tab shows your agency's subscription status and payment history with Visiting Systems.

10. Reports & Audit Log

The Completed tab shows all visits that have been checked out, searchable by date range, client, or caregiver.

The Audit Log (under Settings) records every administrative action taken in your account — who did what and when. This log is read-only and cannot be edited.

11. Configuration

Go to Settings → Configuration to manage agency-wide settings:

  • Agency name — displayed on invoices and reports.
  • Timezone — default timezone for scheduled visits.
  • EVV settings — GPS tolerance radius for check-in location verification.
  • Notification preferences — email alerts for missed check-ins and exceptions.

12. User Management

Go to Settings → Users to view all admin users in your account. Contact your system administrator to add or remove admin accounts or to reset passwords.

Tip: Each caregiver has their own login. Caregivers are managed from the Caregivers section, not from Settings → Users.

13. Subscription & Billing

Visiting Systems bills your agency monthly based on your subscription tier. Your subscription details — plan name, active caregiver count, renewal date, and payment method — are visible under Settings → Subscription.

To upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription, contact Visiting Systems support at support@visitingsystems.com.

14. Getting Help

Additional guides are available in Settings → Help & Docs.

For technical support, email support@visitingsystems.com. Include a description of the issue and, if possible, a screenshot.

Tip: The Audit Log is often the fastest way to understand what happened in your account if something looks unexpected.
